About GMS

GMS is a customised portal with an aim to redress grievances of the employees of the organization. Grievances can be lodged at any place and will land in admin's account. Grievances can then be sent by the admin to the officer of the concerned department.

  • Grievance Redressal platform available online is a comprehensive solution to streamline the complaint handling.
  • Grievances can be lodged by Employees and those complaints will be addressed by Admin and Sub-admin.
  • Facility to upload grievance document.
  • Once a grievance is registered, system will generate a unique complaint number and employees can also view the acknowledgement of the complaints in their account.
  • View current status of grievance.
